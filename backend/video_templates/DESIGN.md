# HisabVani Video Design

## Style Prompt

Warm editorial finance storytelling for Indian households. The video should feel
like a beautifully printed household ledger brought to life: tactile paper,
confident typography, precise data, and restrained cinematic motion.

## Colors

- Ink: `#17130F`
- Paper: `#F7EEDC`
- Saffron: `#D97706`
- Marigold: `#F4B942`
- Forest: `#065F46`
- Muted stone: `#8A7F73`

## Typography

- Display and monetary figures: Fraunces, 800-900
- Labels and supporting text: Work Sans, 350-700

## Motion

- Editorial push transitions are the primary language.
- Warm light leaks mark important reveals.
- Entries should feel decisive, with varied direction and easing.
- The final frame settles rather than bouncing.

## What Not To Do

- No purple gradients or generic neon fintech styling.
- No centered stack of small web-sized cards.
- No equalizers, waveform graphics, or music-note decoration.
- No jump cuts between scenes.
- No low-contrast text over textured backgrounds.
